DSO server: End of alpha test phase 0

2024-12-14

Fellow citizens of Tyr!

I am pleased to announce that Phase 0 of the alpha testing has successfully fulfilled its purpose and is now coming to an end. A substantial amount of valuable data has been collected, paving the way for further improvements to the project.

Tomorrow, Sunday, 2024-12-15, will mark the final day of this phase. After that, the main server, along with the two private test servers, will be shut down. Development will continue with renewed focus and the insights gained during this phase.
As a token of appreciation, the Combat Mode will be enabled on the main server for the last day of testing. This is a small teaser of upcoming features planned for future versions.
Stay tuned for updates, and thank you all for your participation and support of the project.

Even in the harsh deserts of Athas, your efforts shine brighter than the Crimson Sun!

Now rest, citizens of Tyr. Tomorrow, the ziggurat awaits!
