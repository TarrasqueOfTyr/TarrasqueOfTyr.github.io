Dark Sun: Wake of the Ravager, speed issue and music playlist fix patcher

2024-05-12

I’ve added my Dark Sun 2 patcher to the download section.

This patcher addresses speed issue that occur in Dark Sun: Wake of the Ravager on some modern configurations, including those running DOSBox.
The patch works by capping the FPS within the DS2 graphics engine.
Additionally, it fixes a long-standing bug in the music engine that prevented powerful DJ subsystem from functioning as intended. This subsystem dynamically creates playlists for each region, including ambient tracks for Forest and dungeons - content that was previously inaccessible due to the bugs.
It supports all known versions of Dark Sun: Wake of the Ravager:

  • v1.1 CD-ROM edition
  • v1.01 CD-ROM edition
  • v1.0 CD-ROM edition
  • v1.1 Floppy edition
  • v1.0 Floppy edition (Spanish)
  • v1.0 Floppy edition (20.09.94)
  • v1.0 Floppy edition (16.09.94)

You can download it from the download section.
